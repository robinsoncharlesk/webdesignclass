AI-Assisted Website Redesign

Use AI to create a cool and ethical website redesign project!

Objective

In this project, you'll use AI tools to help redesign a website. You'll focus on creating awesome content and designs while keeping things ethical and legal. This means thinking about copyright, giving proper credit to AI-generated stuff, and making sure everything you create follows the rules. This project helps you learn how to use digital tools safely and responsibly, aligning with ISTE Criteria 10.

Steps

Task: Use an AI tool like ChatGPT to create content for two parts of your website, such as the "About Us" page and product descriptions.

Refinement: Personalize and tweak the AI-generated content so it matches your website's style and voice.

Research: Check if the AI-generated content has any copyright restrictions. Think about how to keep the content ethical and legal.

Deliverable:
  • Submit both the original AI-generated content and your customized final version.
  • Reflection Requirement: Write a short reflection about the legal and ethical aspects of using AI-generated content. Explain how you made sure your content follows ethical and copyright rules.

Task: Use an AI tool to come up with a headline, a tagline, and a short introduction for your website's homepage. Make sure to adjust the copy so it reflects your unique brand voice.

Learn: Learn how to give proper credit for AI-generated content on your webpage. Discuss when and how to attribute, based on the AI tool's guidelines.

Deliverable:
  • Submit both the AI-generated and your final edited versions of the homepage content.
  • Include a short paragraph explaining how you decided to attribute the AI-generated content, based on ethical guidelines and your research on copyright.

Task: Use AI tools like DALLÂ·E or ChatGPT to generate design ideas for your website. This could include layout ideas, color schemes, or graphics. Make sure any media you use follows Creative Commons or public domain licensing.

Investigate: Look into how AI-generated images are licensed. Decide if you can use, edit, or share them for your project.

Deliverable:
  • Submit a mood board or screenshots of your design inspirations, highlighting the AI-generated elements.
  • Reflection: Write a short explanation of how you checked that the AI-generated elements are legally usable. Mention any challenges you faced in ensuring the media complies with copyright or licensing rules.

Task: Create a fun and engaging presentation (like slides, a video, or even a comic strip) that shows how you made sure everything in your redesign is ethical. Include:
  • How you gave credit for AI-generated content.
  • Information about the licenses for any AI-generated images or design elements you used.
  • A reflection on how AI plays a role in intellectual property and how you'll use AI tools responsibly in future projects.

Assessment Criteria

  • Effective Use of AI: Did you use AI tools effectively to help create content, code, or design?
  • Ethical Compliance: Did you use AI-generated or sourced content ethically and give proper credit?
  • Reflection Quality: Do you show a clear understanding of AI ethics, intellectual property, and licensing?
  • Web Design Quality: Is your webpage functional, visually appealing, and ethically sound?

Extension Ideas

  • Advanced AI Use: Try using AI tools to automatically check for copyright compliance or to create accessible alt text for images.
  • AI Ethics Debate: Participate in a debate about whether AI-generated content should be considered intellectual property or if it should be freely available for public use.
